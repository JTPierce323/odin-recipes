A Glass of Milk

Description:

Milk is a drink that can be poured in a glass. It makes bones stronger due to the calcium found in milk. Milk can come from a few different sources such as Cows, Nuts, Goats, etc.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Glass
  • A steady hand

Steps:

  1. Put the glass on the counter
  2. With your steady hand, grasp the glass in one hand and the milk in the other hand.
  3. Pour the milk into the glass
  4. Slurp
