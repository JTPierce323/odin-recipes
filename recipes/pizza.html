Pizza

Description:

Pizza is a very tasty dish that originates from Italy. It normally consist of sauce on dough with toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Sauce
  • Dough
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Put sauce on the pre-made dough
  2. Add cheese on top of the sauce
  3. Place in oven for 25 minutes at 350 degrees (F)
  4. Cook until crust is golden brown
